12th Generation Intel Core Processors

The 12th Gen Intel® Core™ processor family will include 60 processors, set to power more than 500 designs across all PC product segments – from desktop gaming to ultra-thin-and-light laptops. 12th Gen Intel Core processors are the first based on Intel 7 process technology and the new performance hybrid architecture to enable major performance gains across a wide range of PC workloads.

At Intel Innovation, Intel unveiled the 12th Gen Intel Core processor family with the launch of the unlocked “K” desktop processors, including the world’s best gaming processor, the 12th Gen Intel Core i9-12900K. With a max turbo boost of up to 5.2 GHz and as many as 16 cores and 24 threads, the unlocked desktop processors reach new heights of performance for enthusiast gamers and professional creators.
